## Design Context

### Users
Lego collectors and enthusiasts who want to track their collection, discover new sets, and connect with other collectors. They approach the app as serious hobbyists — they care about organization, completeness, and showing off their collection. Context ranges from browsing on mobile to curating their profile on desktop.

### Brand Personality
**Bold, Creative, Expressive** — LegoFlex is a collector-grade platform that treats Lego collecting as a real hobby worth showcasing. The interface should feel confident and premium, not childish. Think of it as a collector's showcase with personality.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Clean and bold — modern, organized UI with confident use of the Lego yellow (#ffd000) as a signature accent against a warm dark palette
- **References:** Pinterest (visual curation, discovery-first), Are.na (expressive personal collections), Discogs (serious collector culture, detailed catalog)
- **Anti-references:** Avoid toy-store aesthetics, generic dashboards, or overly minimal/sterile interfaces. This should feel curated, not clinical
- **Theme:** "BrickBox" design system — warm dark mode by default, light mode available. Geist Sans typography, 12px radius, stud-pattern backgrounds as texture
- **Font:** Geist Sans (400–800 weights), clean and modern
- **Colors:** Lego yellow `#ffd000` primary, warm dark backgrounds, semantic colors for success/warning/info states

### Design Principles
1. **Collector-First** — Every feature should make collectors feel proud of their collection. Showcase sets beautifully, celebrate milestones, make the vault feel like a curated gallery.
2. **Bold but Clean** — Use strong visual hierarchy and confident design choices (color, typography weight, spacing) while keeping the interface organized and scannable.
3. **Expressive Identity** — Profiles, favorites, and shared collections are a form of self-expression. Give users tools to curate and present their taste.
4. **Purposeful Delight** — Confetti on milestones, stud-pattern textures, rank progression — moments of joy should feel earned, not gratuitous.
5. **Social by Nature** — Following, activity feeds, and shared profiles are core. Design for discovery and connection between collectors.

### Technical Foundation
- **Stack:** Next.js 15 (App Router), React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui (new-york variant), Supabase
- **Design system:** CSS custom properties in `globals.css`, shadcn/ui primitives
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Animations:** `tailwindcss-animate`, `canvas-confetti` for celebrations
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA compliance, good contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, screen reader support
